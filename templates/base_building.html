{# === HOW THRIVE WORKS ============================================== #}
■

HOW THRIVE WORKS

Each base runs a set of departments. Every department has a thrive score from 1 to 10 that starts at 1 and is raised or lowered by the facilities you build, the equipment you install, the location type, and the base's living conditions. The final score is clamped to the 1–10 range.

A department only exists on a base if something on the base contributes to it (otherwise it isn't shown). The thrive score converts to a dice modifier applied to that department's downtime rolls.

▸ KEY POINT  — facility and equipment bonuses hit specific departments, but GLOBAL CONDITIONS (housing, medical, amenities, power, fringe) move every department on the base at once. That's how housing, medical, and recreation "lift the whole base."

{# === GLOBAL CONDITIONS ============================================ #}
■

GLOBAL CONDITIONS — AFFECT EVERY DEPARTMENT

These modifiers are added to every department's thrive score on the base. This is the only way to lift the whole base at once.

▸ HOUSING  — only matters once a base exceeds 12 space and has at least 5 facilities/workspaces. Scored from the highest Living quality built: none = −1 (−2 on bases of 11+ facilities); Basic = −1; Nice = neutral; Luxury = +1.

▸ MEDICAL  — a base with 8+ facilities and no medical facility takes −1.

▸ AMENITIES  — on bases of 6+ facilities, the share that are General areas + Recreation is measured: under 10% gives −1 (poor amenities); 20%+ gives +1 (good amenities). Between the two is neutral.

▸ POWER  — an isolated base (underwater, orbital, or mobile) with no power plant takes −1.

▸ FRINGE PROJECTS  — each active fringe project hosted on the base is −1, with an additional −1 per assigned child prodigy. Fringe research is powerful but destabilises the base.

{# Recreation call-out — the question this page was written to answer. #}
EXAMPLE — RECREATION: a Recreation facility gives a direct +2 Admin. It does not directly buff the other departments — but it counts toward the amenities share above, so keeping General + Recreation at 20%+ of your facilities grants +1 to every department. That global lift is why building recreation early helps the whole base.
